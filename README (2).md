This Streamlit app allows leadership teams to **explore employee retention risk**, simulate interventions, and generate actionable recommendations based on salary competitiveness and burnout levels.

---

## **Features**

- Select departments and time ranges for analysis.
- Adjust churn drivers: salary competitiveness and burnout environment.
- Simulate salary increases and other interventions to reduce churn.
- Visualize churn risk at both **overall** and **department** levels.
- Receive real-time alerts and actionable recommendations.
- Export department-level churn insights as CSV.
- Metric explanations with hoverable info for clarity.
